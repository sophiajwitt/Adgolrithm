Adgolrithm

A gamefied ad bias auditing experience

Low Fidelity Prototype

Problem

Our goal with this project was to understand and
evaluate the concept of everyday algorithm auditing
whether and how we can empower and incentivize
everyday users to uncover harmful algorithmic
biases in AI systems and assist AI/ML teams in
addressing these issues. We narrowed in on
instagram ad bias specifically.

Methods

Contextual Inquiry

Semi-Structured Interview

Usability Testing

Affinity Diagramming

Storyboarding

Prototyping

Insights & Evidence

Apathy
“I don’t bother reporting ads since ads I see on social media are mostly biased anyways.”
Awareness
“I think if I was more aware about the ads I saw in social media and had a better
understanding of what potential biases there are, I would notice more bias.”
Taking action
“I would be willing to report ads in spite of fear of retaliation if I thought the ad was harmful.”
Motivation to learn
“I like playing short, daily games like crossword, so I think Adgorithm would be a
easy and fun way to learn more about ad bias.”

Solution

We created a gamified experience that will be an engaging method of educating users about algorithmic bias in ads. By creating an “Ad of the Day” game where users can guess targetted demographics in ads. Through participation in this game, we anticipate that increasing awareness about AI bias will allow users to notice biased ads more frequently and be more incentivized to report biased ads.
